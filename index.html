Skills

A basketball dunk is a powerful and athletic move where a player jumps high and forcefully slams the ball through the hoop. It's an exciting and iconic play that showcases athleticism and brings energy to the game.
A layup in basketball is a close-range shot where the player quickly releases the ball off the backboard and into the hoop. It's an essential scoring move used to score points near the basket.
A 3-point shot in basketball is a long-range shot taken from beyond the arc, which is a designated area on the court. It's worth three points and is a valuable scoring option for teams. It requires skill, accuracy, and range to successfully make a 3-point shot.
